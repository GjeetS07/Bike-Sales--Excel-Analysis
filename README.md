# Bike Sales Analysis (Excel Project)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes bike sales data using Excel to understand customer behavior and identify key factors influencing purchase decisions. The goal is to derive insights from customer demographics and sales patterns to support business decision making.

## Tools and Technologies

* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning
* Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization (Charts and Dashboard)

## Dataset Description

The dataset includes customer and sales-related information such as:

* Age and income
* Occupation and education
* Marital status
* Region
* Purchase behavior (bike purchased or not)

## Key Business Questions Solved

### Customer Analysis

* Which age group is most likely to purchase bikes?
* How does income influence purchasing decisions?
* Which occupations have higher purchase rates?

### Sales Insights

* Purchase distribution across regions
* Relationship between marital status and buying behavior
* Demographic trends in bike purchases

## Key Insights

* Customers with higher income are more likely to purchase bikes
* Middle-aged individuals show higher purchase rates
* Certain regions contribute more to overall sales
* Occupation and education level influence buying decisions
